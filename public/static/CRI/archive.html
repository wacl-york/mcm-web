Download previous CRI mechanisms

This page provides copies of previous CRI version mechanisms in FACSIMILE and KPP format.

  • Version 2.2.
  • Version 2.1.

CRI 2.2 files have been prepared for UNIX and PC users, and stored in compressed form.

CRIv2.2 mechanism was exported in September 2023.

Select from below according to your hardware type.

PC: cri_2-2.zip
     
UNIX: cri_2-2.tar.gz

Once uncompressed, the archive contains three files.

cri_2-2_kpp_complete.kpp
Complete CRI 2.2 mechanism, including generic rates and inorganic reactions, in KPP format.
cri_2-2_facsimile_complete.fac
Complete CRI 2.2 mechanism, including generic rates and inorganic reactions, in FACSIMILE format.
cri_2-2_fortran_complete.fac
Complete CRI 2.2 mechanism, including generic rates and inorganic reactions, in Fortran format.
